# Challenge: Historic NJ
**Category:** OSINT
**Difficulty:** Hard
**CTF:** Garden State CTF 2026

## 📂 Files Provided
- historic.jpg

## 🧠 Approach / Thought Process
- Considering I knew I would either have to reverse image search the house, or prompt AI, I thought it would be most time-effective to simply prompt an AI of choice, such as Google Gemini.
- I specifically asked the AI: "Using solely this image, and knowing it was taken across the street from an old store (1800s era) in an NJ historic district, locate the store and its corresponding name. If its too difficult, give me a list of the most likely stores that would fit my description."
- The first store that Gemini gave me happened to be the correct one! I also told it to ignore my location in case it was confusing that with the challenge.

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Solution
1. Ask an AI of choice to use the image and come up with the most likely possibilities for an 1800's era NJ store that is located specifically in an NJ historic district. Have the AI generate a list for improved accuracy.
